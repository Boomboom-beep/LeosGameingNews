Gboard's Unusual Shape Keyboard

The new Gboard keyboard has sparked discussions due to its unconventional shape, which some players find quite bizarre. Developed by their Japanese department, the keyboard's design seems to have a playful tone. In the trailer, the creators humorously express their dislike for using keyboards upside down, making it feel like a lighthearted joke. While it's not set to go into production, a 3D model has been released on GitHub for those who want to try it out.
